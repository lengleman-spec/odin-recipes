Nachos

Restaurant-Style Chicken Nachos

Home

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp canola oil
  • 6 green onions, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 shredded, cooked, whole chicken breast
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1/2 package tortilla chips
  • 1 package shredded cheddar/monterey jack cheese blend
  • 1/2 large tomato, diced

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Heat oil in a deep 12-inch skillet over medium heat. Add green onion and garlic; cook until onions are just tender.
  3. Mix in shredded chicken and season to taste with salt and pepper; toss until well incorporated. Mix in salsa.
  4. Arrange tortilla chips on a large baking sheet. Spoon chicken mixtrure over tortilla chips; top with shredded cheese and tomato.
  5. Bake in preheated oven until cheese has melted, about 10 minutes. Remove from heat and sprinkle with green onion before serving.
