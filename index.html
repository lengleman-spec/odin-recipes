About Us

Odin's Taco Shop offers a cozy, no-frills atmosphere, with the air filled with the savory scent of sizzling meats and fresh tortillas. The menu is focused on traditional, hand-crafted tacos, featuring fillings like marinated carne asada, tender al pastor, or slow-cooked carnitas. Toppings include finely chopped onions, fresh cilantro, tangy salsa, and a squeeze of lime, all served on soft, warm corn tortillas.
